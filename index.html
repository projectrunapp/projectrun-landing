ProjectRun Features

Features that you will like

Graphical representation

We provide you with a graphical representation of your running activity, so you can see track and analyze your progress.

Connect with friends

Connect with your friends and share your running activity with them. You can also see their activity and motivate each other.

Path tracking

Track your path and see it on the map. You can also see your friends' paths and compare them with yours.
